## C. Additions I'd recommend for a firm in this line of business

Tailored to a Jamaican real-estate / conveyancing-led practice serving locals **and
the diaspora**. Roughly ordered by impact-to-effort.

1. **Consult deposits at booking** — take a small online deposit to lock the slot.
   Cuts no-shows dramatically and qualifies serious clients. (Phase 4 / WiPay.)
2. **Secure client portal** — clients log in to see their matter status, upload
   documents, and e-sign the engagement letter. Huge trust signal for a law firm.
3. **Digital KYC / AML onboarding** — ID + proof-of-address capture up front, with an
   audit trail. Turns a compliance chore into a 2-minute step. (Owen's a JP — leans
   into the notarial side too.)
4. **Conveyancing matter tracker** — a visible pipeline (Agreement → Deposit → Title
   search → Stamp duty → Registration → Keys) with automatic milestone updates by
   email/WhatsApp. This is the single most reassuring thing you can show a buyer.
5. **Closing-cost / fee estimator** — a quick tool that estimates stamp duty,
   registration and legal fees for a given purchase price. Captures the lead and sets
   expectations. Pairs perfectly with H.O.M.E.
6. **H.O.M.E. e-book + email nurture** — "Buying a Home in Jamaica (from anywhere)"
   as a free download that collects emails and drips helpful tips → consults.
7. **Diaspora pack** — time-zone-aware booking, video consultations, international
   card payments, and notarization-by-mail guidance. Toronto/NY/London buyers are a
   high-value, underserved segment.
8. **Document automation** — generate first drafts of common documents (agreements,
   wills, POAs) from templates to save Owen's time.
9. **Reviews engine** — after a matter closes, auto-request a Google review. Compounds
   trust and SEO.
10. **Content / SEO** — a few authoritative pages on "buying property in Jamaica from
    abroad", "NHT explained", "what conveyancing costs" — exactly what high-intent
    clients search. Feeds the chatbot's knowledge too.
11. **Self-managed pro directory** — realtors, valuators, surveyors manage their own
    profiles; Ferguson Law is the legal hub of the network. (Phase 8.)
12. **Appointment reminders** — automated 24h / 1h reminders. Quietly lifts show-up
    rates.
